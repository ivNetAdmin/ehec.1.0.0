Rachel McEwan

Elective Home Education Consultant

As a parent you want the best education for your child and for various reasons you may be considering home education.

But how do you come to an informed decision about whether this is in the best interests of your child?

As a specialist in Elective Home Education (parents deciding to educate their children at home), I know that making this decision can be fraught with conflicting emotions. It is also a decision that is often set against a backdrop of unsupportive, ill-informed opinions. Home education, like politics usually arouses strong emotions.

As an Elective Home Education consultant, I am here to help you come to an informed, balanced decision about whether home education is right for them.
